Pioneer Park Activities - Project Plan Website

Purpose

  • Provide a list of activities available at Pioneer Park
  • Provide details of each activity type
  • Provide some weather information for park patrons
  • Provide links to buisness/community events available at the park

Scenario Questions

  • What activities can my family do at the Pioneer Park?
  • Are there community events for these activities at the park?
  • Will the weather be good to take my kids there today?
